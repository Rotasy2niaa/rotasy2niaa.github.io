Life Cycle
Mar 7, 2023
This is my AP 2D Art and Design Portfolio 2021

Inspiration

I am interested in the concept of eternity, the life cycle and the relationship between death, decay and rebirth. Is there also beauty in death and decay, as there is in life? In what ways can I express the magnitude of eternity visually? How could I use visual metaphor to illustrate a life cycle? My work uses a lot of mixed media to create it and relies on my knowledge and understanding of Surrealism.
