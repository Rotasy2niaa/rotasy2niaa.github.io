Unspeakable
Mar 8, 2023
This is my AP 3D Art and Design Portfolio 2023

Design Statement

My works explore the ways feelings and emotions can be expressed and communicated through aesthetic ways without spoken language. Part of the reason is that as an introvert, it is often stressful for me to socialize with people. Therefore, I created a series of interactive devices and installations to make feelings and emotions visible and even fun.

How sustain investigation work?

My investigation began with a “flipping toy”(#1-2) that mimics my anxiety about public speaking, then a two-player board game about social distance(#3-6). Later I explored the use of Arduino board and interactive electronic sensors. I combined Arduino programming in building an electronic mood spinny wheel in #7-8, then a “shy plant” that will close once the obstacle is near the ultrasonic sensor(#9-11). At last, I made a “heart home” modeling installation consisted a light belt that can change color based on the heart rate of the player from the heartbeat sensor(#12-15).
