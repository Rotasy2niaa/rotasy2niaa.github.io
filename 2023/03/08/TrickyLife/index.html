Tricky Life
Mar 8, 2023

Introduction

A role-playing game about a girl with DID who runs out of pills during quarantine and hallucinates and decides to run out of the apartment in search of her pills.
CIGA Game Jam 2022 Shenzhen, China spot
当疫情来袭时，Jean Doe不得不被困在自己的出租屋里，一天、两天……直到她分不清生活和自己哪个到底更疯狂。如果生活和你恶作剧，那你也做一些滑稽的事吧。 AD移动 F与物体互动 1-5选择对应物品 可以和物体交互
When the pandemic struck, Jean Doe was trapped in her rented house, one day, two days… until she couldn’t tell which was crazier, her life or herself. If life is playing a prank on you, then do something funny in return. AD moves, F interacts with objects, 1-5 selects corresponding items, and you can interact with objects.
