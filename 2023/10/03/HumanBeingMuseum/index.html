Human Being Museum
Oct 3, 2023
Groupwork from UCLA Virual Immersive Summerschool

Introduction

The Human Being Museum is a virtual immersive experience that enables people to explore various facets of humanity. Unlike VR art museums that are only for visitation, this interactive experience offers players the opportunity to contemplate issues that could lead to human extinction or to reevaluate our identity as human beings.
Position: project leader, programmer, designer, artist.
