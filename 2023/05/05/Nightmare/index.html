Nightmare渊梦
May 5, 2023
Made in 益·游未尽 72-hour Game Jam (Theme: Healing)

Introduction

A 2D platform game about a girl fighting against her fear.
Position: game designer, pixel artist.

Game Setting 游戏背景

Players need to manipulate the protagonist to jump continuously to climb up, and restore memory by picking up diary fragments scattered on the road. On the way, the protagonist will encounter different monsters, and different defeat methods will point to different outcomes, suggesting that the protagonist will choose to abandon himself or bravely overcome when fighting against psychological diseases. This will guide players to learn to win and face the present bravely in the face of fear, loneliness, irritability and other animal-like negative emotions. Because “when you gaze into the abyss, the abyss is also staring at you.” We hope that when players experience our game, they can also heal themselves on the way of climbing like the hero.
在一场梦里主角深陷深渊，由此展开一段单人冒险之路。玩家需要操纵主角不断跳跃来向上爬升，通过捡起路上散落的日记碎片来还原记忆。在途中会遭遇不同的怪物，主角不同的击败方式会指向不同的结局，暗示主角在对抗心理疾病时是选择自暴自弃还是勇敢克服。由此引导玩家在面对恐惧、孤独、烦躁等如猛兽般的负面情绪时，要学会赢难向上，勇敢面对当下。因为“当你凝视深渊时，深渊也在凝视你。”
