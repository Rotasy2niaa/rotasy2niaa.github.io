Noah Had No Wings
May 5, 2023
Groupwork from Artificial-nature Game Jam 2023






Introduction

It’s a simple job, and you just need to equip the visiting animals with disposable wings as requested by them – not to do it. Enjoy the overtime and gossip before the end of the world.
这是一份简单的工作，你只需要按照来访动物们的要求，给它们装上一次性的翅膀——不按照也行。请好好享受末日之前的加班和闲话家常吧。
